What we check

Three decisions people get wrong before money moves.

"I'm about to buy land from someone. I need to know if it's safe."

Property Title & Document Audit

We deploy legal auditors to verify AGIS and FCDA registry records, court litigation history, and C of O authenticity. We don't take the seller's word; we verify the source.

Buyer Risk Report — proceed / pause / avoid

"My contractor says it'll cost ₦40 million. I have no idea if that's realistic."

Construction Cost Audit

A qualified Quantity Surveyor (QS) reviews your contractor's quote against current Abuja market rates. We identify "padding" and hidden costs before you overpay.

Build Cost Preview Report

"My contractor is asking for ₦6 million for stage two. I don't know if the work is actually done."

Milestone & Payment Audit

Don't release funds based on photos alone. We send site engineers to verify that work matches the invoice. Only pay for what is actually built.
