What we do

Propersafe coordinates independent verification of property, land, and construction matters in Abuja, Nigeria, on behalf of buyers. We engage vetted professionals — lawyers, surveyors, quantity surveyors, and site engineers — to carry out agreed checks and we deliver their findings to you in a written report. We work exclusively for the buyer: we are not realtors or developers, we take no commission from any transaction, and we never handle your purchase or contractor funds.

What our reports are — and are not

  • Our reports present the findings of the professionals engaged on your case, with a plain-English assessment, as at the date of the checks.
  • They are provided for your information to support your own decision. They are not legal advice, financial advice, or a guarantee of any transaction's outcome, and they do not replace independent legal representation in a purchase.
  • Findings depend on the accuracy and availability of public records (such as AGIS and court registries) and the information and documents you provide. Records can change after the date of a search.
  • The free pre-payment risk analysis is an indicative, preliminary read based on your self-reported answers and is not a substitute for the verification checks themselves.

Engagement and fees

  • Submitting an enquiry or risk analysis request is free and does not commit you to anything.
  • Before any paid work starts, we confirm the scope, timeline, and fee in writing. Work begins only after you agree.
  • Fees cover the verification work performed. Because checks involve third-party professionals and search costs, fees for work already performed are not refundable.

Your responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information and genuine documents, and tell us about anything material to the checks.
  • Use our service for lawful purposes connected to a genuine property interest.
  • Make your own final decisions. Whether to proceed with, pause, or abandon a transaction is always your call.

To the maximum extent permitted by law, our total liability arising from any engagement is limited to the fees you paid us for that engagement.
